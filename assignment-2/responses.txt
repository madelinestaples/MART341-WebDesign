1. What is a directory, and what is its relationship to files?
A directory is a folder and vice versa. it's relationship to files is described using familial terms, stemming from the root directory to the parent and
child folders.

2. How are VCS useful?
VCS or Version Control Systems are useful for tracking changes on files and easily reverting back to files thus making sharing and collaboration easier.

Skincare and Me

I love skincare because it is a form of self-care. It is a really great way to do something nice for yourself, while also taking care of yourself.




Advice for those new into skincare

Be patient! Start with simple products, a cleanser, moisturizer, suncreen, and a lip balm. Explore what works for your skin and what doesn't. Be mindful of ingredients.
